## 12. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

### 12.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device to:
- Remember your preferences
- Track session information
- Analyze usage patterns
- Improve user experience

### 12.2 Types of Cookies

**Essential:** Required for app function (cannot disable)
**Analytics:** Help us understand usage (can disable)
**Marketing:** Track for advertising (can disable)
**Preferences:** Remember your choices (can disable)

### 12.3 Managing Cookies

- Check browser settings for cookie management
