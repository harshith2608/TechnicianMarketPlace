# Service Warranty & Protection Policy

**Effective Date:** January 17, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 17, 2026

## 1. Warranty Overview

Every service completed through the Technician Marketplace includes a **7-day Service Warranty** to protect customers from defective work or incomplete services.

---

## 2. 7-Day Service Warranty

### 2.1 Coverage Period
- **Starts:** Upon service completion (when technician marks service as done)
- **Duration:** 7 calendar days
- **Ends:** Automatically after 7 days (no warranty claims accepted after this)

### 2.2 What is Covered by Warranty

The warranty covers:

1. **Service Not Completed As Described**
   - Technician failed to complete promised work
   - Only partial completion of promised tasks
   - Work does not match the agreed-upon service description

2. **Defective Workmanship**
   - Work is done incorrectly or unprofessionally
   - Safety hazards created by technician
   - Poor quality that makes the service unusable

3. **Immediate Failure**
   - Service fails within 7 days due to technician's work
   - Examples: Plumbing work starts leaking, electrical work shorts out, repair breaks immediately

4. **Damage Caused by Technician**
   - Technician caused damage to customer property during service
   - Damage from negligence (not accidents due to existing conditions)

5. **Missing Work or Features**
   - Promised features not installed
   - Incomplete installation
   - Missing components or finishing touches

### 2.3 What is NOT Covered by Warranty

The warranty does NOT cover:

1. **Normal Wear & Tear**
   - Degradation over time after service completion
   - Normal deterioration of materials

2. **Customer Misuse**
   - Damage from improper use after service
   - Ignoring usage instructions provided by technician
   - Overloading or abuse of repaired item

3. **External Factors**
   - Weather-related damage (water leaks, flooding)
   - Power surges or electrical fluctuations
   - Natural disasters
   - Acts beyond technician's control

4. **Changes in Conditions**
   - Damage from environmental changes
   - Damage from other service providers or workers
   - Wear due to heavy use immediately after service

5. **Cosmetic Issues**
   - Minor cosmetic defects not affecting functionality
   - Color variations not specified in booking
   - Small dents or marks
   - Paint variations (unless expressly specified)

6. **Lack of Maintenance**
   - Failure to follow post-service care instructions
   - Lack of regular maintenance recommended by technician

7. **Pre-existing Damage**
   - Damage that existed before the service
   - Issues from prior technicians or installations
   - Structural or hidden defects

8. **Third-Party Causes**
   - Damage from other people after service
   - Theft or loss of items

## 3. Warranty Claim Process

### 3.1 How to File a Claim
**Steps:**
1. Open the App → Navigate to "Bookings" → Select the completed booking
2. Tap "Warranty Claim"
3. Select the issue type and provide:
   - Detailed description of the problem
   - Photos/videos showing the issue
   - Proof that issue occurred during warranty period
4. Submit claim

**Deadline:** Claims must be filed within 7 days of service completion. Claims filed after 7 days are automatically rejected.

### 3.2 Claim Documentation
Provide clear evidence:
- **Photos:** Clear pictures of the problem area
- **Videos:** If applicable, show the issue in action
- **Description:** Detailed explanation of what's wrong
- **Timeline:** When the issue was discovered
- **Evidence it's work-related:** How you know it's due to the service

### 3.3 Claim Review Timeline
- **Day 1:** Customer submits claim
- **Day 2-3:** Platform reviews documentation
- **Day 3-4:** Platform contacts technician with claim details
- **Day 5-6:** Technician responds with their explanation
- **Day 7:** Platform makes final decision

**Maximum Resolution Time:** 7 calendar days

---

## 4. Warranty Remedies

### 4.1 Remedy Options
When a warranty claim is approved, Platform offers two remedies:

**Option 1: Service Redo (FREE)**
- Technician returns to fix the issue at no additional charge
- Scheduled within 3-5 business days
- New 7-day warranty applies to the redo work
- Customer not charged for materials or labor

**Option 2: Refund**
- Full refund: If service is completely unusable or technician unavailable
- Partial refund: If part of service was salvageable
- Refund processed within 5-7 business days
- Refund goes to original payment method

### 4.2 Customer Choice
- Customer can choose between redo or refund
- Technician must honor whichever option customer selects
- If technician unavailable for redo, automatic refund is granted

### 4.3 No Additional Charges
- Warranty claims are FREE for customer
- No deductible or hidden fees
- Technician cannot charge for redo work
- Technician may charge for materials IF customer doesn't provide them (pre-agreed)

## 5. Warranty Terms for Specific Services

### 5.1 Plumbing Services
- **Covered:** Leaks, improper installations, non-functional fixes
- **Timeline:** Failure must occur within 7 days of service
- **Not Covered:** Damage from freezing, corrosion over time
- **Redo Includes:** Pressure testing, proper sealing

### 5.2 Electrical Services
- **Covered:** Non-functional installations, safety hazards, improper wiring
- **Timeline:** Failure must occur within 7 days
- **Not Covered:** Surge damage from external power sources
- **Redo Includes:** Proper testing and verification

### 5.3 Carpentry/Construction
- **Covered:** Structural issues, improper installations, safety problems
- **Timeline:** Must become apparent within 7 days
- **Not Covered:** Cosmetic wear, wood expansion due to humidity
- **Redo Includes:** Proper fastening, finishing

### 5.4 Cleaning Services
- **Covered:** Incomplete cleaning, missed areas, damage during cleaning
- **Timeline:** Must be evident immediately or within 7 days
- **Not Covered:** Quick re-soiling by customer use
- **Redo Includes:** Same cleaning service repeated

### 5.5 General Services
- **Covered:** All work quality issues per Warranty Policy
- **Timeline:** Issues evident within 7 days
- **Not Covered:** Issues from customer abuse or lack of care

## 6. Special Warranty Cases

### 6.1 Multiple Issues
- If multiple issues exist, customer can claim all in one warranty claim
- Each issue evaluated separately
- Partial refunds offered if some issues qualify and others don't

### 6.2 Partial Service Completion
- If only part of service was completed, warranty applies to completed portion
- Non-completed portion may be refunded separately

### 6.3 Complex Issues
- For issues requiring inspection, Platform may send representative to assess
- Assessment fee covered by Platform (free for customer)

### 6.4 Technician Unavailable
- If technician unavailable for redo within 7 days, automatic full refund granted
- Customer not required to wait beyond reasonable timeframe
