# Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

**Effective Date:** January 20, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 20, 2026

---

## 1. Cancellation Policy Overview

This policy governs how customers and technicians can cancel bookings, and what charges apply.

**Key Rule:** Cancellations made **within 4 hours of booking** are **FREE** (100% refund). Cancellations after 4 hours but before service incur a **20% fee** (80% refund). Cancellations during service or no-shows result in **no refund**.

---

## 2. Customer Cancellations

### 2.1 Cancellation Windows & Charges

| Time Window | Cancellation Type | Customer Refund | Fee | Technician Gets |
|-------------------|------------------|-----------------|--------|---------|
| **0-4 hours after booking** | Full Refund | 100% of service charge | ₹0 | ₹0 |
| **4 hours to 1 hour before service** | Partial Refund | 80% of service charge | 20% (split 50/50) | 10% of charge |
| **Less than 1 hour before or during service** | No Refund | ₹0 | 100% | 100% of charge |
| **No-Show** (customer unavailable) | No Refund | ₹0 | 100% | 100% of charge |

**Examples:**
- Service: ₹1,000 booked at 10:00 AM
  - Cancel by 2:00 PM (4 hours) → **₹1,000 refund** (100%)
  - Cancel at 3:00 PM (5 hours, 1 hour before) → **₹800 refund** (80%), Platform & Tech split ₹200
  - Cancel at 3:45 PM (15 mins before) → **₹0 refund**, Technician gets ₹1,000
  - Customer no-show → **₹0 refund**, Technician gets ₹1,000

### 2.2 How to Cancel

**Steps:**
1. Open App → Go to "Bookings"
2. Select the booking to cancel
3. Tap "Cancel Booking"
4. Select reason (optional, helps us improve)
5. Confirm cancellation
6. Refund processed automatically based on window

**Confirmation:**
- Instant confirmation with refund amount displayed
- Technician notified immediately
- Refund initiated to original payment method

### 2.3 Refund Timeline

**For Full Refunds (0-4 hours after booking):**

- Refund initiated immediately
- Money back in 5-7 business days

**For Partial Refunds (4 hours to 1 hour before):**
- 80% refund initiated immediately
- 20% fee retained (10% to technician, 10% platform)
- Money back in 5-7 business days

**For No Refunds (Less than 1 hour before or during):**
- No refund issued
- Full amount goes to technician
- Booking marked as cancelled

## 3. Technician Cancellations

### 3.1 Cancellation by Technician

| Timing | Technician Action | Customer Refund | Technician Penalty |
|--------------|------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Any time before service** | Cancel booking | 100% refund | Warning & rating impact |
| **During service** | Must complete or refund | 100% refund | Severe penalty & suspension risk |
| **No-Show** (Tech unavailable) | Never responded | 100% refund + credit | 10% penalty + potential suspension |

### 3.2 Technician Cancellation Process

**If technician needs to cancel:**
1. Open App → Bookings
2. Select booking → "Cancel Booking"
3. Provide reason (mandatory)
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Customer immediately gets 100% refund regardless of timing

**Automatic Rescheduling:**
- Platform offers to match with another technician
- Customer may accept or keep the refund
- Priority matching with similar-rated technicians
- Original 4-hour refund window resets

### 3.3 Technician No-Show Consequences

**Repeated No-Shows:**
- 1st No-Show: Warning & ₹500 penalty
- 2nd No-Show: 7-day suspension
- 3rd No-Show: 30-day suspension
- 4th No-Show: Permanent removal from Platform

**Penalties:**
- 10% of booking amount charged as penalty
- Negative review impact
- Customer receives 100% refund + ₹100 platform credit
- Report to licensing authorities (if applicable)

## 4. Rescheduling (Alternative to Cancellation)

### 4.1 Rescheduling Instead of Canceling

If customer needs to postpone, **rescheduling is better than canceling**:

**Benefits:**
- No cancellation charges
- 4-hour refund window resets from new booking time
- Technician remains committed
- Same quality service guaranteed

### 4.2 How to Reschedule

**Steps:**
1. Open App → Bookings → Select booking
2. Tap "Reschedule" (instead of "Cancel")
3. Select new date/time
4. Technician notified of new time
5. New 4-hour refund window starts from new booking creation time

5. Technician must accept new time
6. No charges applied

### 4.3 Rescheduling Rules

**Allowed:**
- Reschedule to any future date within 30 days
- Multiple reschedules allowed (no limit)
- Free for both customer and technician

**Not Allowed:**
- Cannot reschedule to same time (that's not a reschedule)
- Cannot reschedule more than 30 days out
- If more than 30 days, must cancel and rebook

### 4.4 What if Technician Doesn't Accept New Time?

- Technician has 24 hours to accept/decline
- If declined, customer can:
  - Pick another time
  - Reschedule with different technician
  - Cancel and receive full refund

## 5. Weather & Emergency Cancellations

### 5.1 Force Majeure Events

**Services may be canceled for "Acts of God":**
- Extreme weather (heavy rain, flooding, storms)
- Natural disasters (earthquakes, etc.)
- Government restrictions (lockdowns, emergency orders)
- Power outages affecting large areas

**In These Cases:**
- Either party can cancel without penalty
- Full refund issued to customer
- Rescheduling offered at no charge

### 5.2 How to Report

**Customer or Technician reports:**
1. Go to Booking → Select booking
2. Tap "Report Emergency/Force Majeure"
3. Provide evidence/details
4. Platform reviews and approves
5. Cancellation processed without penalty

## 6. Legitimate Cancellation Reasons

### 6.1 Accepted Reasons (No Judgment)
- Change in plans
- Scheduling conflict
- Budget change
- Service no longer needed
- Booked wrong service
- Found alternative service provider

**Note:** Platform does NOT judge reasons. All cancellations follow the time-based policy above.

### 6.2 Fraudulent Cancellations (Subject to Review)
Patterns of abuse may result in:
- Account warnings
- Service restrictions
- Account suspension
- Investigation for fraud

**Examples:**
- Systematic cancellations to abuse cancellation windows
- Attempting to manipulate refunds
- Cancellations immediately after technician acceptance

---

## 7. Special Cases

### 7.1 Group Bookings

If booking includes multiple services:
- Each service has independent cancellation window
- Can cancel individual services within their time window
- Discounts may be affected if services canceled

### 7.2 Recurring Bookings

If customer has weekly/monthly recurring bookings:
- Can cancel future bookings anytime
- Cancellations don't affect past bookings
- Early cancellation of plan available

### 7.3 Prepaid Packages

For service packages or prepaid hours:
- Cancellation refunds unused portion
- Used services are not refunded
- Package rate discounts may be recalculated

## 10. Dispute & Appeals

### 10.1 If Customer Disagrees with Charges

**Process:**
1. Customer submits appeal within 7 days of cancellation
2. Provide evidence/explanation
3. Platform reviews cancellation logs
4. Decision within 3 business days
5. Refund issued if appeal is valid

### 10.2 If Technician Disagrees with Cancellation

**Process:**
1. Technician submits dispute within 3 days
2. Provide evidence why cancellation was unfair
3. Platform mediates between parties
4. Final decision within 5 business days
