## 3. Booking & Cancellation Policy

### 3.1 Booking Process
1. Customer selects service and technician
2. Customer provides detailed location and service requirements
3. Technician accepts booking
4. Customer pays booking amount (or completes payment process)
5. Booking confirmed

### 3.2 Cancellation by Customer
**Cancellation Windows:**
- **Free Cancellation:** Up to **2 hours before** scheduled booking time
- **With Charge:** Less than 2 hours before booking time
  - 50% of service charge is deducted (within 2 hours)
  - 100% charge is due if customer is not available at booking time (no-show)

**Process:**
1. Customer initiates cancellation in the App
2. Refund initiated based on cancellation window
3. Technician is immediately notified

### 3.3 Cancellation by Technician
- Technician may cancel with advance notice
- Cancellation fee: Waived (no penalty for technician, customer not charged)
- Platform will help reassign technician or offer full refund to customer

### 3.4 No-Show Policy
- **Customer No-Show:** 100% service charge is retained by technician
- **Technician No-Show:** Customer receives full refund + 5% bonus credit on next booking
- No-shows without valid reason may result in account warnings or suspension

### 4.1 Service Warranty Period
**All completed services include a 7-day warranty period** from the completion date.

**What is Covered:**
- Service was not completed as described in the booking
- Work quality issues that emerge within 7 days
- Service-related damage (technician negligence)

**What is NOT Covered:**
- Normal wear and tear
- Customer misuse after service completion
- Issues from external factors (weather, power fluctuations, etc.)
- Cosmetic defects not specified in the booking
- Damage from other causes after service

### 4.2 Warranty Claims Process
1. Customer contacts Platform with photos/evidence within 7 days
2. Platform reviews claim and contacts technician
3. Technician offered chance to fix issue at no extra charge
4. If technician unable/unwilling, Platform facilitates refund/resolution
5. Resolution decision made within 48 hours

### 4.3 Service Remedies
Platform offers two remedy options for failed services:
- **Service Redo:** Technician returns to fix the issue (free of charge)
